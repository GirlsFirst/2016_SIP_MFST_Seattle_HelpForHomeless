Resources Available

Elizabeth Gregory Home

Elizabeth Gregory Home (EGH) serves single homeless women in the greater Seattle area by providing transitional housing, a day center and case management services. Elizabeth Gregory Home provides a welcoming and respectful refuge where homeless and at-risk women have access to compassionate care.

Seattle Homeless Shelters & Services For The Needy

This shelter is located in Seattle WA, The shelter is for women, man, and for youth.We also provide other homeless resources such as transitional resources for the homeless. Homeless clinic and treatment center resources are also provided on the right navigational bar.

The Sophia Way

The mission of The Sophia Way is to help end homelessness for adult women in King County by providing shelter, life skills training, social services and supportive permanent housing, offering a path from homelessness to stable independent living.

Emergency Shelter

Our overnight shelters provide a safe place to sleep each night for menand women experiencing homelessness, and an opportunity to engage withcounselors and access case management resources for employment.
